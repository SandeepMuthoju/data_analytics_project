## Project Title
Shopify Sales and Customer Analytics Dashboard using Power BI

## Introduction
With the rapid growth of e-commerce platforms such as Shopify, businesses generate large volumes of sales and customer data. Understanding this data is essential to identify customer behavior, product performance, and sales trends. This project focuses on analyzing Shopify sales data to gain meaningful insights that support better business decision-making.

## Problem Statement
E-commerce businesses often face difficulties in understanding customer behavior and sales performance due to large volumes of complex data. This makes it challenging to identify patterns and trends effectively. There is a need to analyze and interpret this data to support informed decision-making.
